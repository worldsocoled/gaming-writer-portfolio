> ABOUT THE WRITER_

HELLO, PLAYER! I'M NICHOLE HIGGINS.


I'M A WRITER WITH A PASSION FOR STORYTELLING AND A LOVE FOR ALL THINGS RETRO. MY WORDS TRANSPORT READERS TO WORLDS BOTH FAMILIAR AND FANTASTICAL, MUCH LIKE THE CLASSIC GAMES THAT INSPIRED THIS PORTFOLIO.


WHEN I'M NOT CRAFTING NARRATIVES, YOU CAN FIND ME EXPLORING INDIE GAME DEVELOPMENTS, COLLECTING VINTAGE TECH, OR SAVORING A CUP OF COFFEE WHILE LISTENING TO CHIPTUNE MUSIC.


MY MISSION: TO CREATE IMMERSIVE EXPERIENCES THROUGH THE POWER OF WORDS, ONE PIXEL AT A TIME.
